Speed up the CPU `SquashingScaler` by inlining the robust- and minmax-scaling branches and computing all needed quantiles in a single `np.nanpercentile([lower, 50, upper])` pass. `RobustScaler` (and the inner `_MinMaxScaler`) are no longer used; behavior is unchanged. Halves `fit` time on large numerical tables (e.g. 100k×100: 726 ms → 362 ms).
