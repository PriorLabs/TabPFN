Fixed `AdaptiveQuantileTransformer` silently losing its `output_distribution`, `random_state` and other parameters when cloned by sklearn (e.g. inside `ColumnTransformer.fit`): the `quantile_norm*` presets produced uniform-distributed output instead of normal. All constructor parameters are now visible to `get_params()`, and `fit()` no longer overwrites the configured hyperparameters with their adapted values.
