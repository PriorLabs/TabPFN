Add an opt-in `passthrough_inf` argument (default `False`) to `TabPFNClassifier`, `TabPFNRegressor`, and the finetuned estimators. When enabled, `±inf` values are no longer rejected during `fit()`/`predict()`; they are carried through preprocessing (replaced with `NaN` for the steps that cannot handle them and restored afterwards) so they reach the model, which handles them natively.
