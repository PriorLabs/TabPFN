Fix activation checkpointing being a no-op during finetuning of TabPFN v3 (and v2.5/v2.6), which caused CUDA OOM on long-context training. The legacy `model.recompute_layer` attribute only applied to the v2-base architecture; v3/v2.5/v2.6 honor `PerformanceOptions.force_recompute_layer`, which now flows from `FinetunedTabPFNClassifier`/`FinetunedTabPFNRegressor` (controlled by `use_activation_checkpointing`, default `True`) through `TabPFNClassifier.forward`/`TabPFNRegressor.forward` and `InferenceEngineBatchedNoPreprocessing.iter_outputs` to the model.
